Quick Start

Creating a Template

Select text in your Word document, switch to the Create tab, and click "Replace with Placeholder" to turn it into a {{placeholder}} field. Do this for each field you want to make fillable.

Filling a Template

Open a document with {{placeholder}} markers, switch to the Fill tab, and click "Scan Document." Fill in the form and click "Fill Document" to replace all placeholders with your values.

Resetting

Click "Restore Original Document" to revert the document to its pre-fill state. Note: this also removes any edits you made after filling.

Common Questions

My placeholders aren't being detected

Make sure placeholders use double curly braces: {{name}}. The text inside must be a single word or use underscores (e.g., {{first_name}}). Avoid spaces inside the braces.

Can I re-fill without resetting?

Yes. Just change the values in the form and click "Fill Document" again. DocFill will swap the old values back to placeholders and fill with the new values automatically.

Where is my data stored?

Your field settings (labels, types, date formats) are saved in your browser's local storage. No data is sent to any server. See our privacy policy for details.
